# DialerJazz — Main Goals

> High-level business and product objectives for the DialerJazz project.

---

## Vision

DialerJazz is a premium, open-source power dialer for outbound sales teams. It reimagines the cold-calling workflow with a gamified, card-based UX inspired by dating apps, turning tedious lead lists into an engaging swipe-and-call experience.

## Target Users

- Solo cold callers (primarily India → USA B2B)
- Small sales teams needing a lightweight dialer without enterprise pricing
- Developers wanting a self-hosted, customizable calling solution

## Core Value Propositions

1. **Card-Based Calling UX** — Leads presented as swipeable cards, not boring spreadsheet rows
2. **BYO Telephony** — Users connect their own Telnyx account. Calls billed to their provider, not to us
3. **Open Source** — MIT licensed, fully self-hostable, no vendor lock-in
4. **Premium Design** — "Midnight Emerald" dark theme with glassmorphism, micro-animations, and a polished feel that rivals paid SaaS

## Business Model (Future)

- Core product is free and open-source
- Potential paid add-ons: Team Spaces (multi-user), hosted managed version, premium integrations

## Version Milestones

| Version | Codename | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| **V1** | "First Call" | MVP — Card UX + Telnyx WebRTC + Campaign Dialer + Manual Dialer |
| **V2** | "Machine" | Power Dialer enhancements + Call Recording + Analytics Dashboard |
| **V3** | "Switchboard" | Multi-Provider (Twilio/Plivo) + CRM Integrations + Team Spaces |
| **V4** | "Enterprise" | Predictive Dialer + TCPA Compliance + Mobile App |
